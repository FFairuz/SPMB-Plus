White Design - Keunggulan

  • Clean dan minimalis
  • Professional appearance
  • Easy to scan multiple badges
  • Less visual fatigue
  • Perfect untuk tabel data
  • Print-friendly
  • Modern & trendy

Gradient Design - Keunggulan

  • Eye-catching & vibrant
  • Strong visual presence
  • Perfect untuk hero sections
  • High perceived importance
  • Memorable design
  • Premium look & feel
  • Great for highlights
  • Distinctive appearance

White Design - Kekurangan

  • Less eye-catching
  • Can blend too much
  • Lower visual impact
  • Less premium feel

Gradient Design - Kekurangan

  • Heavy visual weight
  • Can be overwhelming
  • Tiring in large quantities
  • Less suitable for tables
  • Not print-friendly
  • Can distract from content
